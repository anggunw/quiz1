Description

Dumai is a city in Riau province on the island of Sumatra, Indonesia. This city is divided into seven districts. Dumai is an important transport and trade centre, both regionally and internationally, especially to Malaysia. Dumai is rich in oil (pretroleum and palm oil). Dumai is very easily accessible and has some interesting places to be visit. There are also some tasty local food in Dumai.
