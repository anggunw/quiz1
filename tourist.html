Tourist Places

Dumai have several interesting places that tourist can visit! Here is the list:

Teluk Makmur Beach

Exotic beach, a perfect place to take photos and enjoy the scenery. Located in Teluk Makmur, Medang Kampai district.

Bunga Tujuh Lake

A beautiful lake surrounded by forest with a variety of flora and fauna that live in it. Located in Bukit Batrem, Dumai Timur district

Mangrove Forest

A natural forest with dense mangrove trees. Boats can also be rented to see around. Located in Ratu Sima, Dumai Selatan district.

Purnama Beach

One of the most famous tourism place in Dumai. A perfect place to see the sunset. Located in Purnama, Dumai Barat district.

Pelintung Cave

An amazing cave with stalagmites and stalactites. Located in Pelitung, Medang Kampai district.

Lancang Kuning Monument

A famous monument from Dumai located near Bukit Gelanggang Park. There's a mini library inside.

Bukit Gelanggang Park

A park and green space provided by the government as entertainment. Located in Tlk. Binjai, Dumai Timur district.

Marina Beach

A beautiful beach with various games such as flying dish, banana boat, etc. Located in Tlk. Makmur, Medang Kampai district.
